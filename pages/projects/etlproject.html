3-Step ETL

Summary:

Management takes hours to extract data from brand platforms. I needed to deliver a dashboard where the stakeholders can download aggregations from multiple data sources. I utilised native integrations from my dashboarding tool of choice, and used APIs for the rest (Facebook, Instagram, and Shopify). I used Python as the scripting language, and the Pandas package for data transformations.

The resulting program extracted, transformed, and loaded data onto cloud storage within a 5-minute runtime to meet all the data needs. This saved the management team hours of repeated extraction and formatting on Excel sheets, allowing for better and faster business decisions.

Motivation:

Management takes hours to extract data from brand platforms: Meta (paid and organic), Google (SEO & Ads), Google Analytics, and Shopify. They need a tool that can extract data on a weekly basis for the evaluation of channels and sales performance.

Feasibility: Did they really need a customised program?

Considering the number of solutions in the market, there are probaby some that already meets the surface-level need. However, there are customised needs:

  • Specific engagement metrics for social channels.

  • Bespoke requirements to address Shopify's taxonomy of customers.

  • Shop-level engagements have specific analytical processes.

Yes, they do.
